Complete details are in AqualinkD wiki

  • AqualinkD it will be trying to connect to your control panel and self configure
  • In a web browser go to http://aqualinkd.local/ or http://ip-of-machine.running.aqualinkd depending on your network configuration.
    • i.e If you your device/computer supports MDNS, then you can use http://aqualinkd.local/ since AqualinkD will create a MDNS entry
    •  
  • Go to aqmanager by selecting the 3 lines in top left corner
    •  
  • In aqmanager, once AqualinkD has finished self configuring, expand the "Config" section and select "edit config"
  • If you have no connection or errors, you may need to change the serial interface
    • Toggle the advanced settings, and change serial port appropriately
  • Check your panel type is correct.
    • Must be in format XX-N YYYY (XX=RS or PD, N=Circuits, YYYY=Combo or Only or Dual) example RS-6 Combo
      • Panel size: (or circuits). ie 4, 6, 8, 12 or 16 relates to RS4, RS6, RS8, RS12 or RS16.
      • Panel style: Combo=Pool & Spa, Only=Pool or Spa, Dual=Pool & Spa as separate water bodies
      • Examples :-
        • RS-8 Combo
        • RS-2/14 Dual
        • RS-8 Only
    • If you change the panel type to you panel you must hit save and reload config before changing anything else
  • Go down to the button section and configure your circuits / buttons, labeling them as you see fit
  • Check / change and other values and select "Save" (even if you didn't change anything)
  • Select Restart AqualinkD
