Sometimes it's necessary to see and find ID's that the Jandy control panel can & can't use. AqualinkD's serial logger is designed to do this for you. It does however have many other uses.
  • Find what RS485 devices your Jandy panel can/does support
  • Find all equipment (Jandy & Pentair) on RS485 bus
  • Debug equipment problems, ie SWG stopped being controlled by Jandy panel
  • Decode messages for future support / development of new equipment

It can be run on the command line (details in the above linked WiKi) or through AqualinkD's management interface as shown below.

Running Serial Logger will give you a list of ID's that are in use and ID's that are free (that AqualinkD can use). If you want the complete list of everything, select the Debug option before running.
